About us

Gagan Chauhan

Since 2003

Hello! I'm Gagan, a passionate traveler and photographer based in San Francisco. With a background in computer science, I spend my weekdays coding and problem-solving as a software engineer. On weekends, you'll find me exploring new hiking trails, capturing breathtaking landscapes, and sampling exotic cuisines. I'm a firm believer in the power of technology to shape a better world, and I'm always seeking ways to combine my tech skills with my love for adventure.
Fuelled by curiosity, I'm on a mission to learn something new every day. Whether it's mastering a new coding language or uncovering hidden gems in my city, I thrive on embracing the unknown and turning it into an exciting journey.

A drone is an unmanned aircraft. Drones are more formally known as unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) or unmanned aircraft systems. Essentially, a drone is a flying robot that can be remotely controlled or fly autonomously using software-controlled flight plans in its embedded systems, that work in conjunction with onboard sensors and a global positioning system (GPS).
